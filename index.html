How it all
works together

Organic + Boosted Posts + Meta Ads + UGC = compounding growth at every stage of the funnel.

Top of Funnel Awareness
Organic Lifestyle reels, educational posts, brand story content
Boosted Boost high-performing reels to expand reach beyond followers. Great for page growth.
Meta + UGC Broad cold audience campaigns (longevity, wellness, biohacking). Video view objective to build warm audiences. Creator intro content — authentic first impressions of TruSpan.
Mid Funnel Engagement
Organic UGC reposts, community polls, product education, testimonials
Boosted Boost top UGC posts to extend social proof to engaged followers.
Meta + UGC Retarget video viewers & profile visitors. Carousel ads to product pages. Lookalike audiences. Creator review content & before/afters shared natively.
Bottom Funnel Conversion
Organic Limited promos, direct CTAs, product launch content
Boosted Boost promo posts to followers with strong discount/CTA content.
Meta + UGC Retarget website visitors & cart abandoners. Conversion-objective ads. A/B test creative to lower CPA. Affiliate link content — every sale tracked to the creator.
